Coverage Options Built for Real Families

Life Insurance with LTC Riders

  • Leverages existing life insurance policies
  • Provides tax-advantaged care funding
  • Protects death benefit if care isn't needed
  • Often more affordable than standalone policies
  • Works alongside other planning strategies

Long-Term Care Insurance

  • Dedicated coverage for care-specific expenses
  • Flexible benefit periods (3 years, 5 years, unlimited)
  • Covers assisted living, nursing, and in-home care
  • Premium stability once locked in
  • Tax-favorable treatment under HIPAA guidelines

Medicaid Asset Protection

  • Strategies to preserve family assets for care
  • Maintains inheritance while qualifying for benefits
  • Works with Georgia Medicaid guidelines
  • Requires advance planning (look-back period considerations)
  • Protects spouse's assets and lifestyle

Veterans & Survivor Benefits

  • VA Aid & Attendance benefits for qualified veterans
  • Significant monthly support for care expenses
  • Often overlooked but transformative resource
  • Determines eligibility and support structure
  • Integrates with other planning tools

What You Should Be Thinking About Right Now

First: take a breath. Families navigate this transition every day, and there are established processes that work.

Second: this is the time to start conversations with your parent (if possible), siblings, and trusted advisors. What are their preferences? What are the financial realities? What matters most to them — independence, proximity to family, access to specific services?

Third: don't rush facility tours or decisions. A structured approach — understanding costs, visiting a few options, checking credentials — removes a lot of the stress and tends to lead to better outcomes.
